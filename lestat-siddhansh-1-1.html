2.The principle

Google is one bucket, and it is capped. Best case it delivers 400–700 incremental NCs/week. The channel is constrained by branded search volume, and we cannot create search volume through the channel. Search volume for our top brands is structurally declining (LMNT −10% YoY and at 82–87% impression share — there is nothing left to buy). VMS demand overall is exploding (Amazon EU5 >£3bn, +25% YoY) — it just isn't showing up in Google search.

Everything below is therefore about creating demand, not harvesting it — executed at the same level as the core funnel.

3.The four bets

Bet 1 — Meta as a demand-gen engine

Owner: Leo
Meta is the one major channel that improved efficiency while being grown the least. The opportunity is structural — mix and creative, not budget.
Why now
  • Paid portfolio at diminishing returns overall: spend +65% YoY for +50% customers
  • Today paid social only targets converters — no demand-generation engine exists
  • Healf Zone proven as the standout incremental NC driver; scaling aggressively in Q3
Lines of attack
  • Influencer whitelisting — paid social through creators' own accounts (Asprey list; new influencer hire ramping)
  • Single-brand ads and Healf multiproduct formats
  • Custom landing pages — already converting well above PDPs (Mary Ruth's B2G1, Brecka Essentials)
  • Geist live ~20 Jul (Healf Bloods, sauna blanket) — held to a 2.0+ incremental iROAS bar
  • Scale to 1.5x spend at current efficiency (Leo's Q3 goal)
Funded by: existing PM budget + affiliate reallocation

Bet 2 — New brands as an acquisition engine

Owner: Siddhansh + Conor (BP)
New brands run 40.7% NC share vs ~28% site-wide. Every NC dip this quarter was brand-led; every surge was launch-led. This is our most reliable NC engine — and we are currently throttling it.
Why now
  • NC surges: COROS, SURI, BON CHARGE, H2Tab, Thorne. NC dips: MaryRuth's, Nordic Naturals, Form Nutrition fading
  • Exclusives are acquisition without CAC: Omnilux (exclusive UK launch, brand gifting 150 influencer units), Balance Phone (hands over its entire UK demand, ~10–15k NCs/yr)
  • Self-funding: listing fees run £15–20k per brand
  • Tension to resolve: buying is narrowing onboarding while we ask for +9k NCs/month; high-SV brands sit gated (Zima Dental, 20k+ monthly SV, on Hide)
Actions
  • Reverse the onboarding narrowing — apply an NC-opportunity-cost lens to curation decisions (with Conor)
  • Fix the launch machine: opening POs sized to demand (Theralogix lost 95%+ of launch demand to stockout), ads live <12h from launch
  • Protect the exclusivity ladder: Momentous, Thorne, Element, Mary Ruth's
  • Weekly new-brand NC tracking in the measurement layer
Funded by: listing fees — net positive

Bet 3 — Awareness at net-zero cost

Owner: Siddhansh + Oscar
Awareness is 5–6% vs iHerb at 20% in London. The funnel converts once people know us — awareness is the binding constraint on every paid channel.
Why now
  • Brand search volume peaked Nov 2025 and has been flat since — NC correlates directly with it
  • Brands with London retail presence carry ~3x brand awareness
  • 60% of HX ticket buyers were not existing customers — events acquire
Actions
  • Flagship campaign rebuilt around curation (no talent fee) — measured on NCs, not reach; production-to-media ratio fixed at 25:75 or better
  • OOH as a repeatable pillar: take 30 margin and double the boards — JCDecaux resale model, brand-funded (£400–600k partner target)
  • Retail flagship P&L this quarter, for Q1 2027 launch
  • HX cadence to 4–6 events/year, with the content engine ready before the event, not after
Funded by: brand partners — target net-zero cost to Healf

Bet 4 — New channels portfolio

Owner: Siddhansh + Adrian (carve-out)
A portfolio of small, hard-gated tests on channels we have never touched. Each gets a 6-week budget and a measurement plan. No signal by end of Q3 — it dies.
The portfolio
  • Podcast advertising — genuinely untested; health/wellness podcast audiences are exactly our buyer
  • Direct mail — to cart/checkout abandoners as an NC channel
  • Tube / OOH transit — meeting in motion
  • TikTok — undiscussed to date; creator-led, not brand-account-led
  • Radio — discount-ladder mechanic for measurement
  • Creator storefronts — affiliate storefronts; external advisor's top recommendation
Discipline
  • Every test carries a voucher-code or geo measurement plan from day one
  • Calibration deadline end of Q3: no return, no renewal
Funded by: £200–250k carve-out (agreed in principle with Adrian)
