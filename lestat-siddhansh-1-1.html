2.The principle

Google is one bucket, and it is capped — constrained by branded search volume we cannot create through the channel. Everything else below is about creating demand, not harvesting it.

3.Already in motion

Google

Leo + Mai · +500–800 NCs/wk
  • LTV bidding rolling out account-wide — proven vs POAS: NC purchases +25%, nCAC −21%. Trade-off is explicit: comes at the expense of EC volume
  • Next layers: NCV bidding retest, push generics, WasteNot EC exclusions, subs pricing to next ~750 SKUs, pricing tests on Momentous / Transparent Labs / Nordics / Codeage

4.The bets

Bet 1 — Meta: double down

Owner: Leo
Meta is the one major channel where efficiency has improved while spend has barely scaled. Double down.
  • Scale creative volume and spend
  • Influencer whitelisting — paid social through creators' own accounts
  • Custom landing pages — converting above PDPs but rollout running behind; push here
  • Geist partner test live

Bet 2 — New acquisition brands

Owner: Siddhansh + Conor (BP)
100+ brands launched since December, running 44% NC share vs ~28% site-wide. Keep pumping this funnel.
  • Keep the new-brand funnel pumping — more launches, prioritise exclusive high-NC brands
  • Measure on NC revenue, not NC count — Balance Phone, Blueair, Lifepro are high-AOV brands; counting heads undersells them. A change to drive with BP
  • Google Trends search data is live and loaded as of today — set up a session to go deep on the trends; fundamental to how we pick brands

Bet 3 — Brand marketing

Owner: Siddhansh + Oscar
Awareness is 5–6% vs iHerb at 20% in London. The funnel converts once people know us — awareness is the binding constraint on every paid channel.
  • Underground advertising — tube campaign, measured with voucher codes
  • OOH as a repeatable pillar — brand-funded; take lower margin and double the boards
  • Flagship campaign — rebuilt around curation, measured on NCs not reach
  • Top-of-funnel and mid-funnel ads on paid social and YouTube — the needle-mover to test
  • Guerrilla marketing initiatives — shortlist in progress

Bet 4 — New channels

Owner: Siddhansh
Two channels we have never touched. Small budgets, measured from day one, killed by end of Q3 if no signal.
  • Podcasts — health and wellness podcast audiences are exactly our buyer
  • TikTok — creator-led, not brand-account-led

5.Tactical — end of July

Run a sale at the end of July, timed to payday. Margin is at 35.9% — above the 35.5% target, so we have the headroom — and we typically see a strong NC uplift around payday.
